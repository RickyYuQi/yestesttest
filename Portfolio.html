Basketball

The sport I always adored was Basketball, I grew up in a household where my older brother and Dad loved and played it vigorously. My Dad is a huge NBA fan and played a lot in in his younger years as well as playing here and then when older, my older brother is also passionate and is addicted to NBA and still plays nearly daily. I followed them in their passion for Basketball and have played since. In the Phillipines, Basketball is a main sport and almost everyone plays or knows about it, they would hold many big practice sessions in our school and I would always participate and have an amazing time playing and learning Basketball. When I moved to Australia, I still held onto it, playing everyday after school and would join teams to play games. I stopped playing for teams in year 11 as I needed to put more focus on VCE and found another hobby of working out to accompany it. I had to make more time to going to the gym and my basketball team wasn't winning as often, so I made the choice to leave the team. I still follow NBA and always look back to the days I played with my friends in the afternoon and having tons of fun, this hobby has done so much for me. My favourite player is Derrick Rose as I love his playstyle and how explosive he can be when he drives for a dunk or lay-up. He is extremely fast and good at mid-air situations which led to me idolizing him.
